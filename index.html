BREAKING Meituan Launches LongCat-2.0 — World's First Trillion-Parameter Model Trained on 100% Domestic Chips

Chinese food delivery giant Meituan unveiled LongCat-2.0, claiming it is "the industry's first trillion-parameter model to complete end-to-end training and inference on a 50,000-chip domestic compute cluster." The model's performance is said to be comparable to Google's Gemini 3.1 Pro. This is a milestone in China's push for chip self-sufficiency — most domestic models still rely on smuggled Nvidia chips. Meituan began exploring domestic chip training in 2023. The chipmaker used was not disclosed.

BIG Alphabet's $84.75B Equity Raise Closes — Largest AI Infra Financing in History

Alphabet's equity capital raise closed today at $84.75 billion — upsized from the initial $80 billion. Structure: $30B underwritten offering, $40B at-the-market program (starting Q3 2026), and $10B private placement to Berkshire Hathaway. Proceeds go to AI compute, data centers, and global capacity. Sundar Pichai told investors demand for AI solutions currently exceeds available compute supply. Alphabet's 2026 capex: $180-190B. The raise was oversubscribed.

BERKSHIRE Berkshire Hathaway Invests $10B in Alphabet at Discount

Berkshire Hathaway's $10 billion private placement in Alphabet ($5B Class A at $351.81, $5B Class C at $348.20) is one of its largest single tech investments. The logic: Alphabet generates ~$174B operating cash flow, has a $460B cloud backlog, and reaches 2 billion consumers with Gemini. Buffett entered after Alphabet's June 18-24 talent exodus wiped $269B from market cap, creating a discount entry point.
